What's Cicada

"cicada" is a streetwear-based fashion house, born from bold creativity and a passion for pushing limits. We are rewriting the rules of streetwear. Embrace the fusion of construction, artistry, and fearless attitude in every stitch.

Specializing in gender-fluid, men's, and women's ready-to-wear apparel, "cicada" pushes boundaries with its deconstructive aesthetic and experimental designs. Join us as we redefine Indian street style with a nod to art and culture. Step into "cicada’s" world where crossing boundaries is celebrated, not feared.
